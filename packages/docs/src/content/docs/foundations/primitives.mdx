Per the Constitutional design matrix, **Tier 1 (Primitive)** tokens represent raw, un-opinionated values. They are the complete palette from which all design is drawn but carry absolute zero semantic intent natively.

## Raw Palette Structure

Primitives are prefixed by `--nir-` internally:

1. **Spacing Grid:** A rigid 4px multiplier scaling from `1` (4px) to `16` (64px) preventing random spacing permutations.
2. **Radius Variants:** Granular definitions mapping `xs` through `full`.
3. **Color Logic:** Complete baseline hex bounds (`blue.600`, `neutral.900`) stripped of contextual mappings like 'Success' or 'Danger'.

> [!WARNING]
> DO NOT reference Primitive variables directly in components (e.g. `color.blue.500`). This breaks **Parivartan-sheelta (Radical Adaptability)** by hardcoding identity. Always utilize **Tier 2 Semantic Variables**.
