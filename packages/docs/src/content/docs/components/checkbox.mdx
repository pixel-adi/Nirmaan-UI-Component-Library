Checkboxes manage logical combinations. In the Constitution, checkboxes do not exist in a vacuum; they must comply rigidly with bounding area constraints.

## 1. Accessibility Specification
- **ARIA:** Applies contextual `aria-checked` and relies explicitly on visually associated labels bounding click radius.
- **Min Touch Bounds:** The bounding padding physically extends the element's actual surface to capture generic 44px interactions without resizing the visual SVG square.

## 2. Token Binding
- **Selection State:** Resolves squarely to `color.primary.default`.
- **Disabled State:** Applies strict constraints over `opacity.disabled` bounding over `color.interaction.disabled-bg`.
